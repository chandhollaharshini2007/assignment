Transaction Cards

DEBIT CARD

A debit card is a payment card issued by a bank that allows you to make purchases and withdraw cash by directly deducting funds from your checking account.

It acts as a digital alternative to cash,letting you spend only the money you currently have without incurring debt.

CREDIT CARD

A credit card is a plastic or metal card issued by financial institutions that allows you to borrow funds up to a pre-approved limit to make purchases or withdraw cash.

It acts as a short-term, revolving loan where you must repay the borrowed amount, often with interest if not paid in full by the due date.
